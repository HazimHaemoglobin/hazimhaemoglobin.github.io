TEDx with a difference. World-class thinkers and doers gather in Cambridge to share their work and their vision. And we are here to ensure that these big ideas produce a lasting impact. Join us for an unforgettable event on March 14th 2015.
WHAT IS TEDx?

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxCambridgeUniversity, where x = independently organised TED event. At our TEDxCambridgeUniversity event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.

LAST YEAR’S EVENT

Our theme for last year’s conference was ‘One World’. Weaving together performing arts, global health, the environment and social entrepreneurship, we explored the different ways in which people are interconnected across space and time, and what some of the consequences of this growing interconnectedness might be. Each of our speakers, in their own way, had been involved in developing new, innovative and challenging ways of thinking about this interconnectedness. Our aim was to bring this phenomenal range of speakers together with a diverse audience for a day of engaging dialogue, fresh inspiration and revolutionary ideas.

APPLY FOR TICKETS

Applying for tickets is pretty easy. Just visit our Eventora page by clicking the button below, and fill out the registration form. There’s just one question that’ll require a little cognitive processing on your part, and that’s as follows:

What do you think you have to offer to the other attendees at TEDxCambridgeUniversity? What do you hope to gain from attending yourself? (Max 250 words)

And that’s it! We’ll be looking through all the applications in a few weeks’ time and will let you know either way as soon as we can. Good luck!
